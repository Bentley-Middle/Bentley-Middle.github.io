Conclusion

Is there any reason to these conspiracies? As mentioned before, it offers as an escape from reality. However, it can stray quite far from reality, which in some cases can be harmful. Many popular conspiracy theories have large followings of devoted believers despite the strong evidence against them and in some cases complete debunking of the theory. While conspiracy theories are often fun ideas to think about, they are usually based on little to no truth.
