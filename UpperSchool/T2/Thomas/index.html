Fish

Zebra Danio

The Zebra Danio is a fast fresh-water aquarium fish. They like to be kept in schools of 5 Zebra Danios or more.

Dwarf Gourami

The Dwarf Gourami is a fancy blue fish that is pretty easy to care for. It is not a schooling fish.

"File:Colisa lalia (Neon Dwarf gourami).jpg" by Daiju Azuma is licensed under CC BY-SA 2.5 https://search.creativecommons.org/photos/38407d7e-515d-4ea1-b44e-9d445419562f

Otocinclus Catfish

The Otocinclus Catfish is an algae eater/catfish that will both eat algae on the walls of the tank and clean up waste on the bottom of the tank.
