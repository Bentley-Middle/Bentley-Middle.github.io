"The Three"

Small Forward

The Small Forward, or "the three", is taller than both guards. The small forward is an incredibly versatile position. A small forward does a little bit of everything, they are able to score due to their height and quickness, rebound, pass, and defend. In some cases the small forward facilitates the ball. nown as point forwards, they are small forwards with the role of the point guard. Small forwards are either the secondary or primary scoring option and are responsible for defense. Some NBA small forwards include: LeBron James, Kevin Durant, and Tracy McGrady.
