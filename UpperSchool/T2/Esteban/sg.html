"The Two"

Shooting Guard

The shooting guard, or "the two", is mainly responsible for scoring. They are slightly taller than the point guard and are the secondary ball handler for the team. The shooting guard should be able to score in a variety of ways like shooting, driving, etc. The shooting guard is also proficient in passing and can help the point guard in facilitating if necessary. The shooting guard is typically the most offensively talented player and is thus the team's first scoring option. Shooting guards are very important to closing out games as they are great shooters who also shoot high percentage at the free throw line. Some of the best shooting guards in NBA history include: Kobe Bryant, Michael Jordan, and Klay Thompson.
