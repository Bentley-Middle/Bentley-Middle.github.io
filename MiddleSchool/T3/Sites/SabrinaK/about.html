Sabrina K Designs

About the Owner:

Sabrina was born in California. She started sewing around the age of 8. She continuied to improve the skill, and was able to make some super complex items.

Company History:

Sabrina K Designs was founded when Sabrina was given the opportunity to host a pop up shop for a non-profit girls organization. Sabrina's mom encouraged her to create products revolved around her sewing abilities. She agreed and started off by selling the popular hair accessory; scrunchies as well as colorful hairties.
