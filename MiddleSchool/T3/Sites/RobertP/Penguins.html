Penguins

Penguins are some of the most amazing creatures,being birds who fly through the water instead of the area. Despite living in one of the most inhospitable locations in the world, the arctic, these birds aren't a fearsome predetor(for us at least, not for the fish) but rather a adorable creature which many want as a pet(author included). Some other fun facts about penguins are

  • A group of penguins are called a raft in the water and a waddle on land.
  • The tuxedo of a penguin uses a technique called countershading, where the top of a object is darker than the bottom.
  • Countershading is also used in military camouflage.
  • Penguins live across the Southern Equator, not just the South Pole.
  • Penguins walk up to 60 miles between their breeding and fishing grounds
