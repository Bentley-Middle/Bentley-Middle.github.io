Where to Play Chess

Why Should You Play? How to Play Home Page

Where should you play chess? That is a great question. There are a lot of different websites nowadays where you can play. Some of them cost money, but a lot of them are also free. Here I will list some great websites to start out with.


The Websites


chess.com

This is often a first website people go to. A lot of the features you need a premium membership to use, but you can play unlimited games for free against the computer or other people. If you want the premium membership, the platinum one is probably the best deal. You can customize your board and pieces a lot, and there are a lot of other really cool features you should definitlly check out.


lichess.org

Lichess is another super good site that is pretty common. It is also 100% free, with no ads. Although it has less features and customization, it the fact that it's free completely compensates that. This is another site you defineitally have to check out.

Gothamchess Youtube channel

The Gothamchess Youtube channel is the best chess channel on YouTube, 100 percent. On there are tons of vidoes on chess concepts, games, and top-level tournament recaps. You should defineitally check it out.
