Benefits


There are a lot of benefits to playing chess. There are studies that show it makes you smarter, more creative, and it helps a lot with memory.

It's Fun


Another reason you should play chess is that it's just simply fun. Winning of course feels great, but you can learn a lot from losses too. You probably hear this a lot, but this is true in chess especially. Looking over your games afterward will help you get better.
