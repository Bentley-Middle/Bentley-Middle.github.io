Oakland is a California city on the east side of San Francisco Bay. A statue of the writer, who frequented the region, may be found in Jack London Square. Old Oakland is a nearby neighborhood with restored Victorian homes and boutiques. The Oakland Museum of California, located near Chinatown, covers state history, natural history, and art. The art deco Fox and Paramount theaters, as well as restaurants, clubs, and galleries, are all located in Uptown.
